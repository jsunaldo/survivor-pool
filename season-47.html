How Payouts Work

Every dollar in the pot gets paid out based on your survivor's performance

1st Survivor Eliminated

$30

The very first survivor out of the game — whether voted out, medically evacuated, or quit. If two survivors happen to be eliminated at the same time in the opening episode, the payout drops to $15 per survivor.

Advantage Played

$5

Any time a survivor plays an advantage — whether it works or not. This includes hidden immunity idols, steal-a-vote, extra vote, knowledge-is-power, etc. Does not include forced plays like "Shot in the Dark," "Do or Die," or advantages survivors are required to use.

Individual Immunity Win

$10

Each time your survivor wins an individual immunity challenge. This stacks — a survivor who wins multiple individual immunities earns $10 per win for each of their two paired players.

Runner-Up (2nd Place)

$100

The survivor who finishes in 2nd place at the Final Tribal Council. If two survivors tie for 2nd place, the payout is split between their respective players.

🏆 Winner (1st Place)

$255 each

The Sole Survivor takes home everything left in the pot after all other payouts are made. This season's winning survivor paid out the amount above.

Important: Every payout goes to both players paired with a survivor, independently and in full. For example, if your survivor wins individual immunity, you and the other player assigned to that survivor each receive the full $10. The same goes for the runner-up ($100) and winner payouts. You're not splitting with your co-player — you each earn it separately.
